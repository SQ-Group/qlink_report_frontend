Privacy Policy

Kinship Employee Engagement App

Introduction

At Kinship, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your data while using the Kinship Workplace Companion app, which offers features such as voice-based concern reporting, event updates, surveys, instant alerts, café ordering, and leave calendars. By using the Kinship app, you consent to the practices described in this policy.

Information We Collect

We collect various types of information to enhance your experience with the Kinship Employee Engagement app:

  • Name: Used for app functionality, linked to your identity.
  • Email Address: Used for app functionality, linked to your identity.
  • Photos or Videos: Used for app functionality, linked to your identity (e.g., for event participation or profile content).
  • Usage Data: Data regarding your interactions with the app, including voice submissions, event participation, survey responses, alerts viewed, café orders, and leave calendar checks.
  • Device Information: Information about the device you're using to access the app, such as the operating system, device type, and browser type.
  • Location Data: With your consent, location data for features like event reminders and café ordering.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide, personalize, and improve the app's features and services.
  • To send you notifications related to your participation in surveys, events, or other app activities.
  • To analyze usage patterns and improve app performance.
  • To communicate with you regarding updates, new features, or promotional materials (if applicable).
  • To comply with legal obligations or respond to lawful requests.

How We Protect Your Information

We implement industry-standard security measures to safeguard your data. These measures include encryption, secure server storage, and authentication protocols. However, please note that no method of data transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

Sharing Your Information

You have certain rights regarding your personal data, including the right to access, correct, or delete your information. You can update your personal information directly within the app or contact our support team for assistance.

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to improve your experience with the Kinship app. Cookies help us remember your preferences, analyze app usage, and personalize content. You can manage cookie preferences through your device settings.
